The Person

From Chiro's hills to the
frontier of African AI.

Born in Dire Dawa. Raised in Chiro, West Hararghe — a place that teaches you early: the world won't come to you. You go to it.

Yahya moved to Addis Ababa in his early twenties with a laptop, a conviction, and zero blueprints. He built everything from the ground up — across seven years that took him from self-taught builder to one of East Africa's most recognized AI researchers.

Today he leads international developer teams across the continent, runs AI research on some of the region's most ambitious projects, and has his eyes set on the Gulf.
